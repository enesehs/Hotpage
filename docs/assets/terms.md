# Content Policy

**Effective Date:** January 6, 2026  
**Last Revised:** January 6, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Content Policy ("Policy") establishes the terms and conditions governing user-generated content, permissible use, and prohibited activities within HotPage (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension," "we," "us," or "our"). This Policy applies to all users of the Extension and must be read in conjunction with our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

By installing and using the Extension, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with this Content Policy.

## 2. Scope of Application

### 2.1 Covered Content

This Policy applies to all user-generated content within the Extension, including but not limited to:

- Custom shortcuts and quick links
- User-uploaded background images
- Sticky notes and personal notes
- Todo list entries
- RSS feed URLs and sources
- Custom theme configurations
- Secret links and bookmarks
- Search queries and preferences

### 2.2 Local Storage Nature

All content remains stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to Extension servers. However, users remain responsible for ensuring their locally stored content complies with this Policy.

## 3. Acceptable Use

### 3.1 Permitted Content

Users may store and display content that:

- Complies with all applicable local, national, and international laws
- Respects intellectual property rights and copyright protections
- Serves legitimate personal, educational, or professional purposes
- Does not violate the rights of third parties
- Adheres to community standards of decency and respect

### 3.2 Personal Use Commitment

The Extension is designed exclusively for personal, non-commercial use. Users may:

- Customize their browsing experience through personalization features
- Organize bookmarks, links, and productivity tools
- Access news feeds and information sources
- Manage personal tasks and notes
- Set themes and visual preferences

## 4. Prohibited Content and Activities

### 4.1 Illegal Content

Users shall not use the Extension to store, display, or access content that:

- Violates any applicable laws or regulations
- Infringes upon intellectual property rights, including copyrights, trademarks, or patents
- Contains or promotes child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or child exploitation
- Facilitates illegal activities, including but not limited to fraud, hacking, or identity theft
- Contains malicious code, viruses, malware, or other harmful software
- Violates export control or sanctions regulations

### 4.2 Harmful Content

Users shall not use the Extension to store, display, or access content that:

- Promotes violence, terrorism, or extremist ideologies
- Contains hate speech, discrimination, or harassment based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
- Depicts graphic violence, gore, or disturbing imagery
- Promotes self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
- Contains sexually explicit material or pornography
- Exploits, endangers, or abuses minors in any manner

### 4.3 Deceptive Practices

Users shall not:

- Use the Extension to create, store, or distribute misleading or fraudulent content
- Impersonate individuals, organizations, or entities
- Engage in phishing, social engineering, or other deceptive practices
- Create or distribute spam or unsolicited communications
- Manipulate or interfere with the Extension's functionality

### 4.4 Security Violations

Users shall not:

- Attempt to circumvent security features or access controls
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Extension
- Introduce malicious code or exploit vulnerabilities
- Interfere with other users' access or use of the Extension
- Harvest or collect user data from the Extension or related services

## 5. Copyright and Intellectual Property

### 5.1 User Responsibility

Users are solely responsible for ensuring that:

- All user-uploaded images possess proper licensing or permissions
- Custom links and shortcuts do not infringe upon third-party trademarks
- RSS feed sources are accessed in compliance with their terms of service
- Custom themes and content respect original creators' intellectual property

### 5.2 Copyright Infringement

Users shall not:

- Upload copyrighted images without authorization
- Link to pirated or illegally distributed content
- Circumvent digital rights management (DRM) systems
- Distribute copyrighted material without proper licensing

While the Extension does not host user content on external servers, we respect intellectual property rights. Copyright holders who believe their rights have been violated may contact us at:

## 7. Content Moderation and Enforcement

### 7.1 No Content Monitoring

Due to the local-only nature of data storage, the Extension developers:

- Do not monitor, review, or access user-generated content
- Cannot proactively enforce content policies
- Rely on user self-compliance with this Policy

### 7.2 Violation Reporting

Users who become aware of Policy violations may report concerns through:

- **Email:** enesehs@protonmail.com
- **GitHub Issues:** https://github.com/enesehs/Hotpage/issues

### 7.3 Enforcement Actions

Upon verified reports of Policy violations, we may:

- Issue warnings or guidance to users

- Remove Extension listings from distribution platforms
- Cooperate with law enforcement authorities when legally required

### 7.4 Limitations

Given the local storage architecture, direct content removal is not possible. Users remain responsible for managing and deleting prohibited content from their devices.
